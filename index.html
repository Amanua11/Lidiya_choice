Our Story

A Cafe Born from
Ethiopian Tradition

Lidiya Cafe & Fast Food is more than a restaurant — it is a celebration of Ethiopia's extraordinary coffee heritage, brought into a warm, welcoming space in the heart of Addis Ababa.

From the rich, bold espresso rooted in centuries of Ethiopian tradition to our freshly baked cakes and pastries, every item on our menu is crafted with care and served with the hospitality Ethiopia is known for worldwide.

Whether you are stopping in for a solo breakfast, catching up with friends over coffee, or enjoying a leisurely lunch, Lidiya is your home away from home.

Coffee Culture

Ethiopia: The Birthplace
of Coffee

Coffee was born in Ethiopia. The legend of Kaldi the goat herder discovering coffee beans in the highlands of Kaffa has echoed through centuries, and Ethiopia has been sharing its gift with the world ever since.

At Lidiya, we honour this heritage with every cup. We serve traditional Ethiopian coffee alongside modern espresso preparations — because great coffee deserves great company.
